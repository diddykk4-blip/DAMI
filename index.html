David Aboderin Ministries Int'l (DAMI) is committed to bringing the Gospel into the reality of everyday life. We are dedicated to helping youths and adults find direction, acquire skills, and become relevant leaders in their world.

Led by Dr. David Aboderin, a bondservant of the Lord Jesus Christ, we believe in the power of the Word to transform hearts and homes.
